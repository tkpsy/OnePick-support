# OnePick Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 24, 2025

## Introduction

OnePick ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Photos**: Photos you add to your daily entries are stored locally on your device in the app's container. We do not have access to these photos.
- **Text Notes**: Short messages you add to your entries are stored locally on your device.
- **Dates**: The dates associated with your entries are stored locally.

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Advertising Identifier**: When you use the app with ads enabled, Google AdMob may collect your advertising identifier and device information to serve personalized ads.
- **Device Information**: AdMob may collect technical information about your device, including device model, operating system version, and app usage data.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Local Storage**: All your photos and diary entries are stored exclusively on your device. They are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.
- **Advertising**: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob uses your advertising identifier to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.
- **App Widgets**: When you use our home screen widget, it accesses your locally stored photos to display random memories.

## Data Sharing

- **We do not share your personal data** (photos, notes, dates) with any third parties.
- **Google AdMob**: As our advertising partner, AdMob may collect and process data according to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Your Data Rights

- **Access**: All your data is stored on your device and accessible at any time.
- **Deletion**: You can delete individual entries or uninstall the app to remove all data.
- **Opt-Out of Personalized Ads**: You can disable personalized ads in your iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms.
- Data is shared with the widget extension via sandboxed App Groups for security.
- We do not transmit your photos or diary entries over the internet.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

- **Google AdMob**: For displaying advertisements. [AdMob Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

## Children's Privacy

OnePick does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.
